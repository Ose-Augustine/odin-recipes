CHINCHIN Recipes

Description

This is the recipe for Nigerian chinchin

Ingredients

  • 280g (1 ¾ cup + 2 Tbsp) all purpose flour
  • 82g (⅓ cup) granulated sugar
  • 57g (4 Tbsp) salted butter
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 large egg
  • ¼ cup evaporated milk
  • Oil for deep frying
